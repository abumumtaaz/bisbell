About Us

I would like to take this opportunity to introduce Bisbell Trading & Contracting Company as our esteemed organization. Established in 2005, Bisbell's main activities include civil and building construction, infrastructure and maintenance works. Until 2008, Bisbell kept a moderate scale of projects and work force. Since then, the company has grown rapidly and has become one of the leading Construction Companies in its classification. Bisbell is classified by the Central Tenders Committee as a Grade A building construction contractor.

With the help of a team of professionals experienced in the fields of Management, Construction, Engineering, Planning, Health & Safety, Quality Assurance & Quality Control and Administration, Bisbell has maintained an excellent record of delivering high quality Projects in time and within budget.

In Bisbell we consider Clients and their Consultants as partners and work closely with them to complete and deliver Project in a professional manner and to the satisfaction of all parties.
